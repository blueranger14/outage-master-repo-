Progress

1. Escalated to Sacofa, Field Force and BO team.
2. Sacofa detected multiple fiber fault for our Trunk 1, Trunk 2 and Trunk 3
3. Trunk 1- NMS detected a Loss of Signal (LOS) alarm on the Off Tatau – Nyabau link. Rx Loss is detected at Nyabau (DWDM Trunk 1). Update :- Sacofa FO team still mobilize to site after attending other fault. Trunk 3- NMS detected a Loss of Signal (LOS) alarm on the Selangau T145 – TM Bali (DWDM) link. Update: FO team detected core drop at 19.6km from OYA CTS towards Selangau T145(via SEB 275kv). Trunk 2- SALM-SAGU DWDM Bintulu - Limbang Lawas(Isolate). Update:- FO team inform ETTR 2 hours.
4. Trunk 2 : Alarm cleared but reading laser still in power low due to heavy micro bending at 59km from SAGU. FO team still trying to release micro bending at 59km from SAGU.
Trunk 3: Rectification works a bit delay due to Fiber cable running on Sesco Tower (via SEB 275kv).
Trunk 1: Troubleshooting works still in progress by our team at site.
5. All affected sites recovered. Fault is due to core break at 10km from Oya CTS toward Selangau PS and critical micro-bending at 59.39km from SAGU cabin. Link was normalized after core has been swapped from joint to TB, released the fiber micro-bending and core has been swapped from joint to joint closure.
